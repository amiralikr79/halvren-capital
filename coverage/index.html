Coverage universe

Twenty names we read on a quarterly cadence. Most never become a position. The list is the work.

Halvren covers operators across Canada and the United States in energy, materials, and infrastructure. The list below is the working universe. The Canadian sectors are deepest, the legacy of the firm's home market and the SEDAR+ ingestion layer that has been live the longest. The U.S. universe is in the build, ingested through SEC EDGAR at the same standard. A name appears here when it has earned the read, regardless of whether it has earned a position.

Published

A full writeup is live on the site, with FY data, business analysis, and the Halvren Checklist applied.

On the desk

Actively researching. A public writeup is queued or close. The work happens before the page does.

Watching

Quarterly review. The thesis or the price has not earned active research, but the name remains in scope.

Monitoring

Tracked at a distance. We read the disclosures when they land but have not formed a working view.

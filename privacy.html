Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026 · Effective as of publication

Halvren Capital ("Halvren," "we," "us") operates the website halvrencapital.com. This policy explains what information we collect from visitors, how we use it, and the choices you have. We have tried to keep this short and plain.

What we collect

We collect as little as possible.

  • Standard server logs. Our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically records request metadata such as IP address, user agent, referrer, and timestamp. This is used to operate the service, investigate abuse, and measure aggregate performance. Retention follows Vercel's policies.
  • Email. If you contact us at amirali@halvrencapital.com, your message and email address are retained so we can reply.
  • Newsletter. If you subscribe to our Substack, the signup and all subsequent engagement data are handled by Substack under Substack's own privacy policy. Halvren does not operate an independent mailing list.

We do not run third-party analytics on this site. We do not use advertising pixels, tracking cookies, or session replay.

What we do not collect

  • Financial or account information of any kind.
  • Portfolio holdings, transactions, or positions.
  • Any data tied to an identifiable natural person beyond what you voluntarily send us by email.

How we use what we collect

To operate and secure the website, respond to email you send us, and comply with applicable law. That is the full list.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing. We may disclose information if compelled by a lawful Canadian or Canadian-equivalent process, or to protect Halvren's rights or the safety of others.

Your rights

Under Canadian privacy legislation (PIPEDA and, where applicable, British Columbia's PIPA), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information Halvren holds about you. Write to amirali@halvrencapital.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially we will update the "last updated" date above and, where required, provide notice on the home page.

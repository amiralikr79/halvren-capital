Coverage universe

Every Canadian operator on the desk.

Halvren follows roughly two dozen Canadian-listed operators across energy, materials, and infrastructure. The list below is the working universe. A name appears here when it has earned the read, regardless of whether it has earned a position. Status is honest about depth: a handful are published, more are on the desk, the rest are watched or monitored.

Published

A full writeup is live on the site, with FY data, business analysis, and the Halvren Checklist applied.

On the desk

Actively researching. A public writeup is queued or close. The work happens before the page does.

Watching

Quarterly review. The thesis or the price has not earned active research, but the name remains in scope.

Monitoring

Tracked at a distance. We read the disclosures when they land but have not formed a working view.
